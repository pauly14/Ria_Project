Lisdoonvarna Matchmaking Festival

September 2013

Every year hundreds of singles and thousands of supporters flood the Clare town of Lisdoonvarna in September - to find the partner for life or to simply have a good time. Matchmakers match prospective partners in a time-honored tradition and everybody is happy.

Killorglin Puck Fair

August 2013

A goat is crowned king in the Kerry town of Killorglin and all hell breaks loose - not a plot for a cheap horror flick but a tradition with pagan roots. Still practiced for three days every August, on the 10th, 11th and 12th. Don't miss the election of the "Queen of Puck"!
