Lisdoonvarna Matchmaking Festival

September 2013

Every year hundreds of singles and thousands of supporters flood the Clare town of Lisdoonvarna in September - to find the partner for life or to simply have a good time. Matchmakers match prospective partners in a time-honored tradition and everybody is happy. Or not. Some matches are not made in heaven Guide Review of the Lisdoonvarna Matchmaking Festival.

Killorglin Puck Fair

August 2013

A goat is crowned king in the Kerry town of Killorglin and all hell breaks loose - not a plot for a cheap horror flick but a tradition with pagan roots. Still practiced for three days every August, on the 10th, 11th and 12th. Music, entertainment and pageants range from the traditional to the modern. Don't miss the election of the "Queen of Puck"! More: Puck Fair Website
